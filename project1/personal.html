Academic Ventures:

  • Women in Computing

Women in Computing is an all inclusive organization that promotes and supports women entering computing fields. It acts as a support system, a professional development opportunity, as well as a networking avenue. Currently, I am an active member of WiC and am the student tutor for the organization.

  • WiCHacks

WiCHacks is WiC's all-women's hackathon that is held every year in the winter. Currently, I am director of logistics for the event and am in charge of things like venue, budgeting, and scheduling of the event.

The RIT Theme Park Enthusiasts is an organization of people who are enthusiastic about the Theme Park industry
